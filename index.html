What we do

We host fun, music-fueled night events in Johannesburg South. Safe, social spaces where the community can link up, smoke, drink, play and vibe.

More Than Just Events. It's a Movement.

Buds in a Blunt isn't just about throwing parties. We're about creating a community -- a family of music lovers, stoners, and social souls who know how to have fun the Jozi South way. From the trivia nights to music jams, every event we host is built around good vibes, good people, and good times.

If you're looking for a safe space to vibe with hip hop and amapiano, meet new people, and roll with a tribe that feels like home -- this is where you belong.
